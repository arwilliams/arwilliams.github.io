About me

I am currently a software engineer at Aurora, where I work on developing localization algorithms for autonomous vehicles. I am generally interested in mathematically principled approaches to estimation problems; my primary areas of expertise center around estimation and optimization on manfolds, and particularly on Lie groups.

I did my undergraduate and Master’s studies at CMU in the Department of Mathematical Sciences, and have been working in the software industry (primarily robotics) since.

On this website you’ll find miscellaneous notes and writeups that I’ve found useful in my robotics work and that I think might be useful to others.
