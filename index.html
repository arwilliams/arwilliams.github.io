About me

I am currently a software engineer at Aurora, where I develop state estimation algorithms for autonomous vehicles.

Prior to joining Aurora, I worked at Carnegie Robotics, where I worked primarily on localization and mapping for outdoor mobile robots.

I did my undergraduate and Master’s studies at CMU in the Department of Mathematical Sciences, and have been working in the software industry since.

On this website you’ll find miscellaneous notes and writeups that I’ve found useful in my work and that I think may be useful to others.
